Happy birthday, my love! I am so proud of everything you’ve done so far. You’ve been strong and caring through everything. You’ve worked hard and never gave up. Even when times are tough, you still keep going. I admire that so much. You’re doing great, believe me. It’s okay to take breaks when you need them, you deserve to rest too. Don’t feel bad about it, it’s important to take care of yourself. You matter so much, and you deserve all the love in the world. I’m really proud of you for how far you’ve come. I’ll never run out of ways to show you how much I love you. You are my everything.

The world can be uncertain, but there is one thing I am sure of: it’s you and me. I feel so lucky to have you in my life. You give me comfort when I need it most. You are my light, my guide, and my peace. Together, we’ve made something beautiful, like stars in the dark sky. I will always cherish how we found each other. You make everything feel okay, no matter how hard life gets. You’re always there for me, and I’m so thankful for that. I feel so safe when I’m with you. You complete my heart in ways I never imagined. No matter what happens, I know I will always be with you. I love you more than words can say.

Even though I can’t always find the right words to tell you how much you mean to me, I hope you feel it. You are my love, my home, and my best friend. Every moment with you is special. I want to spend the rest of my life making you feel loved. You are everything I ever wanted and more. I will always be by your side, through good times and bad. I promise to support you no matter what. If the world is being a little mean towards you, please remember that I’m always here for you, okay? You won’t ever find me behind you because I’ll be beside you. That way, you know you can always lean on me as we walk this journey together. I am so proud to love you. Happy birthday again, mahal ko, you deserve all the happiness.

With all my heart,
Dric
