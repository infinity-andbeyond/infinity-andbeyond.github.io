Where My Heart Found Home

To My Raya

A kind of love that doesn’t need convincing, doesn’t come with conditions, and doesn’t ask for explanations. Loving you has never been a question. It’s always felt natural, like breathing. But if I had to try and explain it, if I had to wrap words around the way I feel about you, I’d start here—with everything you are and everything you’ve been to me.

I love you because somewhere in the middle of all my chaos, you made me feel like I was worth loving. You stepped into my world not to fix me, but to hold my hand through it. I love you because even on the days when I felt like I was falling apart, you didn’t flinch. You didn’t run. You stayed, arms open, heart wide, whispering words I couldn’t believe but needed to hear. You’ve seen me in moments I would never show anyone else, and you chose to love me anyway. That means more than I can ever put into words.

I love you because you have this way of making me feel safe—like no matter how loud the world gets, I can always find peace in your presence. I love you because your love doesn’t just exist in the good moments, but especially in the hard ones. You've held me when I was angry, comforted me when I cried, and never made me feel like a burden even when I was at my lowest. You’ve made me feel like I’m not too much, never too complicated, never too broken. You loved me when I didn’t feel lovable—and that kind of love? It stays. It roots itself deep inside.

I love you because of all the little things too—the small gestures, the quiet care, the patience you show when I can’t explain how I feel. I love you because I can laugh with you, cry with you, be completely silent with you, and still feel understood. I love you because when I picture my future, it’s your face I see beside mine. I love you not just for who you are, but for how being with you makes me feel. You bring out parts of me I thought I lost.

And above all, I love you because I can’t imagine losing you. The thought alone breaks me. You are the home my heart found when it didn’t know where it belonged. You are the reason I believe in love, in healing, in second chances. I may not always find the perfect words, but this love I carry for you? It’s constant. It’s deep. It’s real. And I hope you always feel it, even in the quiet, even in the spaces where words don’t reach.
